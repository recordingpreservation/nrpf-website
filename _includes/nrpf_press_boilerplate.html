The National Recording Preservation Foundation (NRPF), a federally chartered corporation, is a charitable, 501(c)3 non-profit organization. The NRPF works across the United States to foster awareness of the diverse perspectives and communities documented in audio, to support the preservation of historical and at-risk audio collections, and to coordinate resources for the digital preservation of audio recordings. The NRPF was founded through the National Recording Preservation Act of 2000.
